Chicken with mushroom sauce

Ingrediants:

Chicken:

  • Chicken thighs (optional- with bone and skin)
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cooking oil
  • Butter

Sauce

  • Garlic, minced
  • Mushrooms
  • Rosemary
  • Butter
  • Cooking cream

Instructions:

Chicken:

  1. Season both sides of the chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Melt 1 tablespoon of butter in a pan over high heat with the cooking oil.
  3. After melting the butter, sear the chicken thighs in the pan with the skin side down for two to three minutes, or until the skin turns golden.
  4. After flipping the chicken and adding the water, cook it covered over medium heat for about 15 minutes, or until the chicken is almost cooked through.
  5. After that, take out of the pan and place aside.

Sauce:

  1. Add the minced garlic and chopped mushrooms to the other tablespoon of butter.
  2. For two minutes, saut√©. Add the Cooking Cream and stir in the rosemary.
  3. Stir continuously until it reaches a gentle simmer.

Assembling the dish:

  1. Once the chicken is thoroughly cooked and the sauce has thickened, return the chicken thighs to the sauce and cook for an additional ten minutes over medium heat.
  2. Serve the steamed vegetables and cooked rice or mashed potatoes alongside the creamy chicken casserole.
